A job is something people do to help others and earn money to buy the things they need. There are many kinds of jobs, like being a doctor, artist, teacher, waiter, cook, or electrician. Let’s take a look at this table:

Job/Occupation Meaning
Pilot 🧑‍✈️ A person who flies aeroplanes in the sky.
Author ✍️ A person who writes books and stories.
Architect 🏛 A person who designs buildings and houses.
Baker 👩‍🍳 A person who makes bread, cakes, and cookies.
Astronaut 🧑‍🚀 A person who travels into space to explore planets and stars.
Judge 🧑‍⚖️ A person who makes decisions in court and helps solve problems.
Job/Occupation Meaning
Photographer📸 A person who takes pictures with a camera.
Scientist 🧑‍🔬 A person who studies and learns new things about the world. They make new discoveries.
Plumber👩‍🔧🔧 A person who fixes pipes and sinks in homes.
Soldier 🪖 A person who helps protect the country, especially during a war.
Vet 🧑‍🔬🐕 A person who takes care of sick animals and helps them get better.
Librarian 👩‍🏫📚 A person who helps people find books in a library.
